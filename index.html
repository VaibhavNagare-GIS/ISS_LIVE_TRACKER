🎯 UN Sustainable Development Goals Alignment

This ISS Live Tracker actively supports four UN SDGs by making real-time orbital science accessible, advancing open geospatial infrastructure, raising climate awareness, and celebrating international space partnership.

04
Quality Education
Interactive STEM tool that visualizes real orbital data for students and educators worldwide, making space science accessible to everyone.
09
Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure
Demonstrates open-source geospatial technology and live API-driven infrastructure, promoting accessible innovation for all.
13
Climate Action
The ISS carries instruments monitoring CO₂, sea levels, ice sheets, and extreme weather patterns critical for climate research.
17
Partnerships for the Goals
Built on 15-nation collaboration between NASA, ESA, JAXA, CSA, and Roscosmos — a model for global partnership in science.
